AnalytiXIN Summer Internships

The Lucy Family Institute for Data & Society invites applications for the AnalytiXIN Summer Internship program to engage with Indiana companies who have committed to growing their digital and analytics capabilities through the development of skilled workforces.

These Central Indiana Corporate Partnership (CICP) companies provide a dynamic experiential learning environment for summer internship opportunities for STEM students to work with industry leaders and to pursue solutions in data science, analytics, and cybersecurity. The AnalytiXIN summer interns will be hosted at the 16 Tech Innovation District or the host Company office in Indianapolis.

The funding for the internship includes a stipend as well as housing support. The internship period will be 8 weeks, starting about June 1st.

iTREDS Faculty Opportunity

Would you like to work with an interdisciplinary team of iTREDS scholars?

iTREDS is an NSF-funded Interdisciplinary Traineeship for Socially Responsible and Engaged Data Scientists program. Students develop skills in the areas of teamwork, working with stakeholders, ethics, communication, and entrepreneurship.

Launched in 2020, the iTREDS program has recruited three cohorts of 72 students (67% female and 19% URMs) from the University of Notre Dame and St. Mary’s College.

We are now announcing a Call for Capstone Project Topics for Cohort #3.

Duration of Capstone: Spring ’23, Fall ’23, and Spring ’24.
Team size: 3-5 (interdisciplinary team of scholars)

Important Dates and Submission

  • Project topics due date: January 20th, 2023.
  • Announcement of students’ project topic matching and team formation: January 30, 2023.

Please let us know if you might be interested in mentoring a team of students for their Capstone project. This is also a fantastic Broader Impact call out for your NSF projects. For additional information, please contact Sugana Chawla, iTREDS Program Coordinator, at schawla@nd.edu.
